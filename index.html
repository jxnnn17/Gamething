Red Cube Route Rush

Build your red cube inside the 9x9 setup grid, then survive level mode or score mode hazards and enemies.

Game Modes

  • Only movement cubes can be attached directly next to the red cube.
  • Special cubes (rocket/piston/stabilizer/gun) attach onto existing side modules.
  • Lasers and enemies can break attachments one-by-one.
  • Reach the goal cube for 5 seconds in Levels, or survive and score in Score Mode.
